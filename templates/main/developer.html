Overview

Project Vastra is an E-Commerce Website under Enactus IIT Ropar which bridges the gap between our project entrepreneurs and buyers by providing a platform for easy selling and buying of clothes.
We the Technical Team of Enactus are a huge supporter of Open Source. We invite all developers across the globe to connect with us and contribute.

How to contribute?

This project is active and needs significant improvements. You can contribute to this project by adding bugs or feature requests at our GitHub Link.
We hope to see you all there!
